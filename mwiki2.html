Question 2 Blog Post

Managing People to Overcome Cybersecurity Attacks from Within an Organization

1. Audit

Audits are an important means to achieve security in organizations. Obviously the actual looking into things inside organizations are important, but also the fact that audits are known to be there can be an effective deterrence.

 

2. Access Control

Access control is a crucial control in cybersecurity. It works such that only the right persons are allowed to have access to certain information necessary for those roles. Together with the principle of least privilege, they can effectively reduce the likelihood of insider threats.

 

3. Management System

A competent management system that consists of the right policies and procedures is the main tool of control for management in organizations. The roles and responsibilities should be explicitly and properly spelled out. Accountability should be incorporated into the system as well.

 

4. Control

Control is important mechanisms in organizations to enforce policies and procedures. Controls can include a large number of formats and methods. When effectively enforced, controls can help maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of information.

 

5. Monitoring

Monitoring is a crucial management control in detecting and responding cybersecurity threats in real time. If something happens, by catching it happens just in time, monitoring can let management to take important actions.
